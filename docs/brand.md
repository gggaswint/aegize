# Brand

The source of truth for how Aegize describes and presents itself. When copy or
design decisions conflict, defer to this document.

## Mission

Build the trust layer for autonomous AI.

> "Trust layer" is the internal north star — the lens for product decisions
> (does this increase understanding, control, reviewability, confidence?). It is
> not a public tagline. Public copy leads with infrastructure.

## Product statement

Aegize is infrastructure for autonomous AI agents.

## Elevator pitch

Aegize is the runtime layer between autonomous AI agents and the tools they
use — providing identity, policy, permissions, approvals, audit logging,
observability, and runtime governance for every AI action.

## Brand personality

- **Calm.** Infrastructure is reassuring, not alarming. We do not raise the
  temperature.
- **Technical.** We speak to engineers in their own terms, with working code.
- **Confident, not loud.** We state what is true plainly and let it stand.
- **Honest.** We describe what exists and admit what does not. We never imply
  more maturity than the project has.
- **Vendor-neutral.** Independent, model-agnostic, and framework-agnostic.

The peer set we measure against: Stripe, Vercel, Cloudflare, Linear, Docker,
OpenTelemetry, Terraform. Serious infrastructure companies — not research labs,
not safety nonprofits.

## Words to use

infrastructure · runtime · governance · identity · policy · permissions ·
approval · audit · observability · trust · deterministic · vendor-neutral ·
declarative · enforcement · boundary · gateway · control · attributable ·
on the record · default deny · agents · actions · tools

## Words to avoid

- **Fear language:** doom, catastrophe, threat to humanity, existential.
- **Safety-nonprofit framing:** "AI safety," "alignment," "guardrails for
  humanity." (Aegize is operational infrastructure, not an advocacy project.)
- **Overusing "security."** Security is one outcome, not the brand. Use it
  precisely (e.g. the security policy / disclosure), not as the headline.
- **Hype:** revolutionary, game-changing, magical, unprecedented, next-gen.
- **Vague AI buzz:** "harness the power of AI," "AI-native," "supercharge."
- Overpromising platform features that do not exist yet.

## Visual language

The visual metaphor is **the trusted passage between autonomous AI agents and
the real world** — a controlled boundary that actions pass through.

Principles:

- Deep, near-black canvas; generous whitespace; precise typography.
- One confident accent color; the green / amber / red verdict colors
  (allowed / approval / denied) used meaningfully, never decoratively.
- A deliberate **sans + mono** split: prose in a grotesque, everything "system"
  (labels, code, status, pipeline nodes) in monospace.
- Subtle motion only. Real artifacts (the runtime pipeline, the terminal demo)
  over decoration.

Avoid, explicitly:

- Generic AI imagery: robots, brains, humanoid figures.
- Shields, padlocks, glowing circuits, neural-net meshes.
- Stock photography, fake enterprise logos, testimonials.
- Purple-on-white gradients and other generic "AI startup" aesthetics.

## Logo direction

The mark should express a **boundary that actions pass through** — a controlled
gateway, not a barrier. Directions worth exploring:

- gateway / portal
- threshold
- bridge
- keystone
- trust boundary
- controlled flow

It should read as a clean, geometric infrastructure mark — closer to a protocol
or a piece of plumbing than to a security badge. No shield, no lock.

### Temporary logo note

The current logo is **provisional**. It establishes a coherent identity for
launch and is expected to be replaced. The final brand mark is TBD; when it is
designed, it should follow the directions above and the visual language here.

## Tone of voice

- Short, declarative sentences. Active voice.
- Lead with what something *does*, then why it matters.
- Prefer concrete nouns (identity, policy, audit) to abstractions
  (empowerment, intelligence).
- Show, don't sell: a working demo beats an adjective.
- Never fear-monger. The reason to adopt Aegize is control and clarity, not dread.
